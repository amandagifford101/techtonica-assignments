Mom's Pesto Primavera

My mom would always cook this pasta with a lot of fresh veggies and parmesan cheese for me and my sister when the time was right.

    Ingredients
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan
  • 5 lbs pine nuts
  • 1 tiny piece of basil
  • a truckload of olive oil
  • a touch of love
Make the Pesto Sauce
  1. Combine parmesan, pine nuts, basil, and olive oil together in food processor, and blend until 110% smooth.
  2. Do some reiki on the mixture.
  3. Let it rest in the moonlight for 3 hours for 3 nights.
Make the pasta
  1. Boil the water until simmering.
  2. Add noodles, hopefully they are bowties, because bowties are clearly the best pasta ever.
  3. Once noodles are tender, combine the sauce and the noodles and feed it to your cats/dogs, and once they are satisfied
    4. with their dinner and are fast asleep, you may take your human self to the dinner table and eat this meal.
